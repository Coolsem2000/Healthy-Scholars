At Healthy Scholars Day Care & Pre-School, we offer a range of programs and services to meet the diverse needs of families in our community:
Daycare for Infants and Toddlers
Provide a safe and nurturing environment for the youngest members (8 months & beyond) of our community.

Preschool for Children
Build a solid foundation for academic and social success. This involves
    KG 1 (1.5 - 4yrs)
    KG2 (4-5yrs)
    KG3 (5-6yrs)
    Grade 1 (6-7yrs)
    Grade 2 (7-8yrs)
    Grade 3 (8-9yrs)


Tuitions
Offer additional support to help students excel in their studies.
Field Trips
Enrich experiences beyond the classroom to broaden horizons. Plan age-appropriate outings to places like local farms, parks, and museums to expand children's horizons and provide real-world learning opportunities.


Extracurricular Activities
Encourage creativity and self-expression through arts and music programs as well as physical education and sports programs to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and leadership skills.
Special Needs Programs
Inclusive programs that accommodate children with special needs and disabilities that can foster an environment of empathy and acceptance by Implementing specialized programs and resources to support children with special needs (autism, down syndrome etc.), ensuring inclusivity and individualized learning.


Environmental and Outdoor Education
Teach environmental stewardship and sustainability practices through environmental education initiatives and outdoor learning experiences that connect students with nature and encourage environmental awareness.
Nutrition and Wellness Programs
Promote healthy eating habits and overall well-being among students.

Parent and Caregiver Support Programs
Engage them through workshops, seminars, and resources that promote their active involvement in their children's education.
Community Service and Outreach
Foster a sense of community and social responsibility through community service and outreach programs.
